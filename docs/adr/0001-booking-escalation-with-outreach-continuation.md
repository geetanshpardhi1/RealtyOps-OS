# Booking Escalation with Outreach Continuation

RealtyOps OS V1 separates booking control from outreach cadence when confidence is low or borderline. We decided that low-confidence booking decisions escalate immediately to a Brokerage Agent, while follow-up outreach can continue on the defined cadence unless a Brokerage Agent/admin pauses automation. This preserves safety for high-risk booking actions without sacrificing conversion momentum from consistent follow-up.
